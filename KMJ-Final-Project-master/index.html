What To Watch?

As a result of COVID-19, more and more people are at home watching Netflix, Amazon Prime etc. We all know the struggle, scrolling for hours to try and find the perfect film to watch. Find out what to binge watch next by checking out our review section here

The FOMO (fear of missing out) is real. If you didn't watch Tiger King during the first lockdown, you will never understand any and all future references to such an iconic series. According to a Radio Times survey, over 42% of us have lied about watching a TV series or film, in order to join in part of a conversation or social situation. You don't need to be that person.

How It Works

So you're probably wondering how this service works? Basically we review any upcoming TV series that we like, or you ask us for, to help make your decisions on what to watch easier. If you can't decide between you and normally spend the whole night deciding, or just simply looking for some new recommendations, then you've come to the right place.
